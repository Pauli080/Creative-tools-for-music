Continue where you left off

Move’s Wi-Fi connectivity means you can easily transfer Sets to Live and Note with Ableton Cloud and Move Manager.

Create and perform with Live

Move is the ultimate compact MIDI control surface for Ableton Live, thanks to tight integration with Live’s interface and workflow.

Connect and play

Plug in headphones, external speakers, microphones and other devices through Move’s 3.5 mm stereo ports. Move can also send or receive MIDI via its USB-A port.

Tactile control

Shape sounds with nine touch-sensitive, endless rotary encoders, and play notes, beats, samples, and clips with 32 backlit pads. Each pad responds to pressure independently, letting you add more movement and depth to your tracks.
